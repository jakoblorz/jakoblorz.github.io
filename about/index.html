Who am I?

My name is Jakob Lorz and I study Economics and Computer Science at TU Darmstadt, Germany.

Currently I am Student Assistant at the Institute of Information Systems & E-Services, where I support PhD Candidates in their work. I focus on web development with user-friendly interfaces as well as scalable and custom backend solutions. I am a digital- and cloud-native developer (JS, Golang, Java/C#, F#, SQL) with creative abilities (Adobe Illustrator & Photoshop).
